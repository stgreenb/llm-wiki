## L1/L2 Routing Rule

- **L1** (auto-loaded every session): Rules, gotchas, identity, credentials, project-specific preferences
- **L2** (on-demand via wiki query): Projects, workflows, research, deep knowledge

Routing: "Would a mistake without this knowledge be dangerous/embarrassing? → L1. Merely inconvenient? → L2."

### Per-Agent Location
- **Claude Code**: `memory/` directory (referenced from CLAUDE.md)
- **OpenCode**: `AGENTS.md` (auto-loaded) + `.opencode/instructions/` for sensitive rules
